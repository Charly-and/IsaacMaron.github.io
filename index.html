Mechatronics2024

Automated Multi-Material Sorting System

A competitive sorting machine that classifies and routes 48 mixed pieces — aluminium, steel, black and white nylon — in under 20 seconds using sensor fusion and custom microcontroller logic.

🏆 1st place — 48 pieces sorted in 17 s (1 error). Fastest recorded time in course history.
Objectives

Classify 4 material types at speed using non-contact sensing. Orchestrate stepper motors, PWM conveyor, and end-gate actuator from a single microcontroller with minimal classification errors.

Technical Details

Inductive proximity sensor for ferrous/non-ferrous detection. Optical contrast sensor for polymer colour classification. Custom interrupt-driven C++ on Arduino coordinating all actuators.

Thermofluids2026

Minifridge Reverse Engineering & COP Optimisation

Full reverse-engineering analysis of a commercial minifridge to diagnose poor thermal performance. Iterative Python model using CoolProp accurately predicted elevated steady-state interior temperatures (~9 °C above spec).

Objectives

Identify root cause of above-spec temperatures. Build a validated thermodynamic model coupling energy balances, mass flow convergence, and Lockhart-Martinelli pressure-drop correlation.

Key Outcome

Novel desiccant wheel proposal driven by 55 °C condenser waste heat — predicted to reduce duty cycle 8–15% by suppressing evaporator frost accumulation at zero operating cost.

Technical Details

CoolProp refrigerant property library. Iterative mass flow convergence loop. Two-phase pressure drop via Lockhart-Martinelli. Full P-h diagram generation in Python.

PythonCoolPropThermodynamic modellingLockhart-MartinelliTwo-phase flow

Capstone2025

Wing Foil Force Measurement System — MECH 499

Instrumented boom handle system for wingfoiling wings that quantifies the pulling forces a rider experiences in real time — enabling objective, data-driven comparison of wing fabrics and construction methods for client Tagmata.

🔌 In progress — developing force quantification platform to replace subjective rider feedback in wing material R&D.
Objectives

Design and build a force-measuring boom handle that captures rider pull forces across multiple axes during real wingfoiling sessions. Provide Tagmata with repeatable, quantitative data to evaluate polyester weaves and competing fabric constructions against one another.

Client & Motivation

Tagmata currently relies on subjective rider feedback to evaluate wing fabrics. This system replaces guesswork with measured force data, giving their R&D team a defensible basis for material selection and marketing claims.

Technical Details

Multi-axis force sensing integrated into a standard boom handle form factor. Data logging for post-session analysis. System designed to survive saltwater, dynamic loading, and the mechanical abuse of active foiling sessions.

Three-Stage EV Gearbox Design — MECH 360

Mechanism Design2024

Motorised Retractable Bridge — MECH 350

Motorised bridge constrained to an 11-inch height envelope, spanning 32 inches and clearing 22 inches on retraction while supporting a 2 kg live load with vehicle passage.

Objectives

Symmetric, predictable extension within tight height constraint. Maximum mechanical advantage at full extension. Support 2 kg live load via bidirectional motor control.

Technical Details

Reverse double four-bar linkage with 1:1 gear-coupled chassis. DC motor to custom gear reduction to cable spool through eyelets. 6 mm birch plywood, laser-cut gears, steel shaft pivots, 3D-printed pins.
